# OPS08-BP03 Analyze workload traces

Analyzing trace data is crucial for achieving a comprehensive view of an application's operational
journey. By visualizing and understanding the interactions between various components,
performance can be fine-tuned, bottlenecks identified, and user experiences enhanced.
Desired outcome: Achieve clear visibility into your application's distributed operations, enabling
quicker issue resolution and an enhanced user experience.

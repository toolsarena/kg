# OPS09-BP03 Review operations metrics and prioritize improvement

Setting aside dedicated time and resources for reviewing the state of operations ensures that
serving the day-to-day line of business remains a priority. Pull together operations leaders and
stakeholders to regularly review metrics, reaffirm or modify goals and objectives, and prioritize
improvements.

# Resources 54

| COST 10: How do you evaluate new services? |
| --- |
| As AWS releases new services and features, it's a best practice to review your existing architect
ural decisions to verify they continue to be the most cost effective. |

| COST 11: How do you evaluate the cost of effort? |
| --- |
| Evaluate the cost of effort for operations in the cloud, review your time-consuming cloud
operations, and automate them to reduce human efforts and cost by adopting related AWS
services, third-party products, or custom tools. |

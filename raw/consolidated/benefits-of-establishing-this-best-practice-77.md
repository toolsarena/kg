# Benefits of establishing this best practice:

• Improved workload reliability: By ensuring that external dependencies are consistently
available and performing optimally.
• Faster issue detection and resolution: Proactively identifying and addressing issues with
dependencies before they impact the workload.
• Comprehensive view: Gaining a holistic view of both internal and external components that
influence workload health.
• Enhanced workload scalability: By understanding the scalability limits and performance
characteristics of external dependencies.
Level of risk exposed if this best practice is not established: High

# SEC01-BP05 Reduce security management scope

Determine if you can reduce your security scope by using AWS services that shift management
of certain controls to AWS (managed services). These services can help reduce your security
maintenance tasks, such as infrastructure provisioning, software setup, patching, or backups.
Desired outcome: You consider the scope of your security management when selecting AWS
services for your workload. The cost of management overhead and maintenance tasks (the
total cost of ownership, or TCO) is weighed against the cost of the services you select, in
addition to other Well-Architected considerations. You incorporate AWS control and compliance
documentation into your control evaluation and verification procedures.

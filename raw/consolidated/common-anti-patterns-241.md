# Common anti-patterns:

• Your organization has a five year plan to migrate all workloads to AWS. The business case for
cloud includes the modernization of 25% of all workloads to take advantage of serverless
technology. The CIO communicates this strategy to direct reports and expects each leader to
cascade this presentation to managers, directors, and individual contributors without any in-
person communication. The CIO steps back and expects his organization to perform the new
strategy.
• Leadership does not provide or use a mechanism for feedback, and an expectation gap grows,
which leads to stalled projects.

# PERF01-BP03 Factor cost into architectural decisions

Factor cost into your architectural decisions to improve resource utilization and performance
efficiency of your cloud workload. When you are aware of the cost implications of your cloud
workload, you are more likely to leverage efficient resources and reduce wasteful practices.

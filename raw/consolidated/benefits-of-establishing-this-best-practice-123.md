# Benefits of establishing this best practice:

• Increased reliability, predictability, and consistency of recovery operations.
• Ability to meet more stringent recovery objectives, including Recovery Time Objective (RTO) and
Recovery Point Objective (RPO).
• Reduced likelihood of recovery failing during an incident.
• Reduced risk of failures associated with manual recovery processes that are prone to human
error.
Level of risk exposed if this best practice is not established: Medium

# OPS10-BP04 Define escalation paths

Establish clear escalation paths within your incident response protocols to facilitate timely and
effective action. This includes specifying prompts for escalation, detailing the escalation process,
and pre-approving actions to expedite decision-making and reduce mean time to resolution

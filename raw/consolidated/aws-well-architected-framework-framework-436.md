# AWS Well-Architected Framework Framework

Automated responses to alarms could include the replacement of failing components, the
adjustment of compute capacity, the redirection of traffic to healthy hosts, availability zones, or
other regions, and the notification of operators.
Desired outcome: Real-time alarms are identified, and automated processing of alarms is set
up to invoke the appropriate actions taken to maintain service level objectives and service-level
agreements (SLAs). Automation can range from self-healing activities of single components to full-
site failover.

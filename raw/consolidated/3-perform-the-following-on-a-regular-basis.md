# 3. Perform the following on a regular basis:

• Evaluate team size and skills as operating conditions and the workload change.
• Adjust team size and skills to match operational requirements.
• Verify ability and capacity to address planned lifecycle events, unplanned security, and
operational notifications through AWS Health.
Level of effort for the implementation plan: High. Hiring and training a team to support a
workload can take significant effort but has substantial long-term benefits.

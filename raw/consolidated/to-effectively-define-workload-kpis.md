# To effectively define workload KPIs:

1. Start with business outcomes: Before diving into metrics, understand the desired business
outcomes. Is it increased sales, higher user engagement, or faster response times?
2. Correlate technical metrics with business objectives: Not all technical metrics have a direct
impact on business outcomes. Identify those that do, but it's often more straightforward to
identify an issue using a business KPI.
3. Use Amazon CloudWatch: Employ CloudWatch to define and monitor metrics that represent
your KPIs.
4. Regularly review and update KPIs: As your workload and business evolve, keep your KPIs
relevant.
5. Involve stakeholders: Involve both technical and business teams in defining and reviewing KPIs.

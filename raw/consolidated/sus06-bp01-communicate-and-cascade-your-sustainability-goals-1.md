# SUS06-BP01 Communicate and cascade your sustainability goals

Technology is a key enabler of sustainability. IT teams play a crucial role in driving meaningful
change towards your organization's sustainability goals. These teams should clearly understand the
company's sustainability targets and work to communicate and cascade those priorities across its
operations.

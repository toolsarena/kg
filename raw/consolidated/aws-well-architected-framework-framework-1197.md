You can achieve dynamic, automated resource scaling in conjunction with IaC and delivery
pipelines. By monitoring key metrics and applying predefined scaling policies, Auto Scaling can

cost-efficiency. This reduces the potential for manual errors or delays in response to changes in
application or workload requirements.
The combination of IaC, automated delivery pipelines, and Auto Scaling helps organizations
provision, update, and scale their environments with confidence. This automation is essential to
maintain a responsive, resilient, and efficiently-managed cloud infrastructure.

# Desired outcome:

• You collect logs from your workload components and AWS service dependencies, and you
publish them to a central location where they can be easily accessed and processed.
• Your logs contain high-fidelity and accurate timestamps.
• Your logs contain relevant information about the processing context, such as a trace identifier,
user or account identifier, and remote IP address.
• You create aggregate metrics from your logs that represent your workload's behavior from a
high-level perspective.
• You are able to query your aggregated logs to gain deep and relevant insights about your
workload and identify actual and potential problems.

# AWS Well-Architected Framework Framework

• Foster a sustainability culture: Use training programs (like AWS Skill Builder) to educate your
employees about sustainability. Engage them in sustainability initiatives. Share and celebrate
their success stories. Use incentives to award them if they achieve sustainability targets.

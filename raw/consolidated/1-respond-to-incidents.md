# 1. Respond to incidents:

• Use insights from observability tools to quickly identify and respond to incidents.
• Implement AWS Systems Manager Ops Center to aggregate, organize, and prioritize
operational items and incidents.
• Use services like Amazon CloudWatch and AWS X-Ray for deeper analysis and
troubleshooting.
• Consider AWS Managed Services (AMS) for enhanced incident management, leveraging its
proactive, preventative, and detective capabilities. AMS extends operational support with
services like monitoring, incident detection and response, and security management.
• Enterprise Support customers can use AWS Incident Detection and Response, which provides
continual proactive monitoring and incident management for production workloads.

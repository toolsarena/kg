# Benefits of establishing this best practice:

• By having well-documented and well-tested emergency access processes, you can reduce the
time taken by your users to respond to and resolve an emergency event. This can result in less
downtime and higher availability of the services you provide to your customers.
• You can track each emergency access request and detect and alert on unauthorized attempts to
misuse the process for non-emergency events.
Level of risk exposed if this best practice is not established: Medium

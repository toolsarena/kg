# Implementation steps

• Subscribe to updates for threat intelligence publications that are relevant to your business and
industry. Subscribe to the AWS Security Bulletins.
• Consider adopting services that incorporate new threat intelligence automatically, such as
Amazon GuardDuty and Amazon Inspector.
• Deploy a fleet management and patching strategy that aligns with the best practices of the Well-
Architected Operational Excellence Pillar.

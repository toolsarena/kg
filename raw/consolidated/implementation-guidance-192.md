# Implementation guidance

Having internal policies in selecting resources and architecture provides standards and guidelines
to follow when making architectural choices. Those guidelines streamline the decision-making
process when choosing the right cloud service and can help improve performance efficiency.
Deploy your workload using policies or reference architectures. Integrate the services into your
cloud deployment, then use your performance tests to verify that you can continue to meet your
performance requirements.

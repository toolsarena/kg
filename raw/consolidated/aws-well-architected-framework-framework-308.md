# AWS Well-Architected Framework Framework

Desired outcome: You verify that only authorized users can access data on a need-to-know basis.
You protect your data with regular backups and versioning to prevent against intentional or
inadvertent modification or deletion of data. You isolate critical data from other data to protect its
confidentiality and data integrity.

# Benefits of establishing this best practice:

• Enhanced workload resiliency: You prevent errors caused by exceeding AWS resource quotas.
• Simplified disaster recovery: You can reuse automated quota management mechanisms built in
the primary Region during DR setup in another AWS Region.
Level of risk exposed if this best practice is not established: Medium

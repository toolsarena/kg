# Implementation guidance

Testing your deployment process is as important as testing the changes that result from
your deployment. This can be achieved by testing your deployment steps in a pre-production
environment that mirrors production as closely as possible. Common issues, such as incomplete
or incorrect deployment steps, or misconfigurations, can be caught as a result before going to
production. In addition, you can test your recovery steps.

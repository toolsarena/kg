# Customer example

AnyCompany Retail defines ownership as the team or individual that owns changes and support
for resources. They leverage AWS Organizations to manage their AWS accounts. Alternate account
contacts are configuring using group inboxes. Each ITSM queue maps to an email alias. Tags
identify who own AWS resources. For other platforms and infrastructure, they have a wiki page that
identifies ownership and contact information.

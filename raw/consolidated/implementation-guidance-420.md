# Implementation guidance

Your deployment pipelines are a critical component of your software development lifecycle
and should follow the same security principles and practices as any other workload in your
environment. This includes implementing proper access controls, validating inputs, and regularly
reviewing and auditing the permissions associated with your CI/CD infrastructure.

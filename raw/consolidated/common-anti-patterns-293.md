# Common anti-patterns:

• Regulatory requirements, market expectations, and industry standards for assurable security are
not well-understood for your business
• Your cybersecurity frameworks and control objectives are misaligned to the requirements of your
business
• The implementation of controls does not strongly align to your control objectives in a
measurable way
• You do not use automation to report on the effectiveness of your controls
Level of risk exposed if this best practice is not established: High

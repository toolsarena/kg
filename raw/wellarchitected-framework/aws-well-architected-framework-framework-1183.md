# AWS Well-Architected Framework Framework

Observability is more than just collecting metrics from workload components and being able
to view and alert on them. It's about having a holistic understanding about your workload's
behavior. This behavioral information comes from all components in your workloads, which
includes the cloud services on which they depend, well-crafted logs, and metrics. This data gives
you oversight on your workload's behavior as a whole, as well as an understanding of every
component's interaction with every unit of work at a fine level of detail.

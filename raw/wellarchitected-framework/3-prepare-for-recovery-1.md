# 3. Prepare for recovery

a. Evaluate the state of your recovery site and deploy critical components to it in advance. For
more detail, see REL13-BP04.
b. Define clear roles, responsibilities, and decision-making processes for recovery operations,
involving relevant stakeholders and teams across the organization.
c. Define the conditions to initiate your recovery processes.
d. Create a plan to revert the recovery process and fall back to your primary site if required or
after it's considered safe.

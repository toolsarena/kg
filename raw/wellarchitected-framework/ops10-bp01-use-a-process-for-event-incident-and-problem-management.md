# OPS10-BP01 Use a process for event, incident, and problem management

The ability to efficiently manage events, incidents, and problems is key to maintaining workload
health and performance. It's crucial to recognize and understand the differences between these
elements to develop an effective response and resolution strategy. Establishing and following a
well-defined process for each aspect helps your team swiftly and effectively handle any operational
challenges that arise.

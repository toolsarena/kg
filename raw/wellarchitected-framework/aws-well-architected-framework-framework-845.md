# AWS Well-Architected Framework Framework

• You are asked to make a change to your security groups, but you are not given any details of
what change needs to be made, what the impact of the change could be on all the workloads,
and when it should happen. The manager forwards an email from the VP of InfoSec and adds the
message "Make this happen."
• Changes were made to your migration strategy that reduce the planned modernization number
from 25% to 10%. This has downstream effects on the operations organization. They were not
informed of this strategic change and thus, they are not ready with enough skilled capacity to
support a greater number of workloads lifted and shifted into AWS.

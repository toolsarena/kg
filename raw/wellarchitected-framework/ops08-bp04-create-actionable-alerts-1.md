# OPS08-BP04 Create actionable alerts

Promptly detecting and responding to deviations in your application's behavior is crucial. Especially
vital is recognizing when outcomes based on key performance indicators (KPIs) are at risk or when
unexpected anomalies arise. Basing alerts on KPIs ensures that the signals you receive are directly
tied to business or operational impact. This approach to actionable alerts promotes proactive
responses and helps maintain system performance and reliability.
Desired outcome: Receive timely, relevant, and actionable alerts for rapid identification and
mitigation of potential issues, especially when KPI outcomes are at risk.

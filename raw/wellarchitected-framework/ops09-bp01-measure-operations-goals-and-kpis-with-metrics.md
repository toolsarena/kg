# OPS09-BP01 Measure operations goals and KPIs with metrics

Obtain goals and KPIs that define operations success from your organization and determine that
metrics reflect these. Set baselines as a point of reference and reevaluate regularly. Develop
mechanisms to collect these metrics from teams for evaluation. The DevOps Research and
Assessment (DORA) metrics provide a popular method to measure progress towards DevOps
practices of software delivery.

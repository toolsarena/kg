# AWS Well-Architected Framework Framework

Benefits of establishing this best practice: Implementing emergency levers can improve
the availability of the critical components in your workload by providing your resolvers with
established processes to respond to unexpected spikes in demand or failures of non-critical
dependencies.
Level of risk exposed if this best practice is not established: Medium

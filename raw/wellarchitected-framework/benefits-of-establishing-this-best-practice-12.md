# Benefits of establishing this best practice:

• Informed decision-making: With insights from telemetry and business KPIs, you can make data-
driven decisions.
• Improved operational efficiency: Data-driven resource utilization leads to cost-effectiveness.
• Enhanced workload stability: Faster detection and resolution of issues leading to improved
uptime.
• Streamlined CI/CD processes: Insights from telemetry data facilitate refinement of processes
and reliable code delivery.
Level of risk exposed if this best practice is not established: High

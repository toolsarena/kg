# AWS Well-Architected Framework Framework

• You lack observability of application components that are not being monitored.
• You focus only on low-level technical metrics and excluding business metrics in your monitoring.
Benefits of establishing this best practice: When you regularly review your monitoring, you can
anticipate potential problems and verify that you are capable of detecting them. It also allows you
to uncover blind spots that you might have missed during earlier reviews, which further improves
your ability to detect issues.
Level of risk exposed if this best practice is not established: Medium

REL02-BP02 Provision redundant connectivity between private networks in the cloud and on-
premises environments
Implement redundancy in your connections between private networks in the cloud and on-
premises environments to achieve connectivity resilience. This can be accomplished by deploying
two or more links and traffic paths, preserving connectivity in the event of network failures.

SEC 3. How do you manage permissions for people and machines?
Manage permissions to control access to human and machine identities that require access to
AWS and your workloads. Permissions allow you to control who can access what, and under what
conditions. By setting permissions to specific human and machine identities, you grant them access
to specific service actions on specific resources. Additionally, you can specify conditions that must
be true for access to be granted.

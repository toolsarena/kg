# Customer example

AnyCompany Retail recently completed a migration of workloads from an on-premises
environment to their landing zone in AWS with a lift and shift approach. They performed an
operations review to reflect on how they accomplish common operational tasks and verified that
their existing responsibility matrix reflects operations in the new environment. When they migrated
from on-premises to AWS, they reduced the infrastructure teams responsibilities relating to the
hardware and physical infrastructure. This move also revealed new opportunities to evolve the
operating model for their workloads.
While they identified, addressed, and documented the majority of responsibilities, they also
defined escalation routes for any responsibilities that were missed or that may need to change as
operations practices evolve. To explore new opportunities to standardize and improve efficiency
across your workloads, provide access to operations tools like AWS Systems Manager and security
tools like AWS Security Hub CSPM and Amazon GuardDuty. AnyCompany Retail puts together a
review of responsibilities and strategy based on improvements they wants to address first. As the
company adopts new ways of working and technology patterns, they update their responsibility
matrix to match.

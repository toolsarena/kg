# REL06-BP06 Regularly review monitoring scope and metrics

Frequently review how workload monitoring is implemented, and update it as your workload
and its architecture evolves. Regular audits of your monitoring helps reduce the risk of missed or
overlooked trouble indicators and further helps your workload meet its availability goals.
Effective monitoring is anchored in key business metrics, which evolve as your business priorities
change. Your monitoring review process should emphasize service-level indicators (SLIs) and
incorporate insights from your infrastructure, applications, clients, and users.
Desired outcome: You have an effective monitoring strategy that is regularly reviewed and
updated periodically, as well as after any significant events or changes. You verify that key
application health indicators are still relevant as your workload and business requirements evolve.

# IAM.

You can use both AWS IAM Identity Center and direct IAM federation simultaneously to manage
access to AWS. You can use IAM federation to manage access to the AWS Management Console and
services and IAM Identity Center to manage access to business applications like Quick or Amazon Q
Business.
Regardless of the sign-in method, it's critical to enforce a strong sign-in policy.

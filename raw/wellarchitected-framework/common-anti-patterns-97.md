# Common anti-patterns:

• Implementing a one-size-fits-all approach to data lifecycle management, without considering
varying sensitivity levels and access requirements.
• Considering lifecycle management only from the perspective of either data that is usable, or data
that is backed up, but not both.
• Assuming that data that has entered your workload is valid, without establishing its value or
provenance.

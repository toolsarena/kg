# Implementation guidance

Chaos engineering provides your teams with capabilities to continually inject real world disruptions
(simulations) in a controlled way at the service provider, infrastructure, workload, and component
level, with minimal to no impact to your customers. It allows your teams to learn from faults and
observe, measure, and improve the resilience of your workloads, as well as validate that alerts fire
and teams get notified in the case of an event.
When performed continually, chaos engineering can highlight deficiencies in your workloads that,
if left unaddressed, could negatively affect availability and operation.

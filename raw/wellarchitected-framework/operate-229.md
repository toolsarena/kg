# Operate 229

| Impact level | Description |
| --- | --- |
| High | Affects many staff or customers, high
financial impact, high reputational damage,
or injury. |
| Medium | Affects a groups of staff or customers,
moderate financial impact, or moderate
reputational damage. |
| Low | Affects individual staff or customers, low
financial impact, or low reputational damage. |

| Urgency level | Description |
| --- | --- |
| High | Exponentially increasing damage, time-sens
itive work impacted, imminent escalation, or
VIP users or groups affected. |
